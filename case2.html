🎯 The challenge

The Polish market needed a different approach

Standard lead generation campaigns in Poland were underperforming. Cost-per-lead was high, lead quality was inconsistent, and there was no automated pipeline to nurture prospects after the initial click. Leads who didn't convert immediately were simply lost.

The insight: Polish audiences respond strongly to value-exchange mechanics. Rather than asking for an email upfront, I designed a gamified campaign where prospects win something first — flipping the psychology from "give us your data" to "claim your prize."

🔨 Process

How I built it — step by step

From audience research to live automation — the full build across 5 weeks.

01
Research

Market & audience analysis

Analysed Polish audience behaviour in Meta Ads Manager. Interactive mechanics outperformed static lead forms by 2–3× in engagement for the student housing vertical.

02
Creative strategy

Localised Polish ad creative

Native-language ad sets targeting students aged 18–26 in Warsaw, Kraków, and Wrocław via Meta and Google Display.

03
Build

Gamification mechanic + landing page

Spin-to-win where every visitor is guaranteed to win — removing the psychological barrier and dramatically increasing email submission rate.

04
Automation

Zapier → Google Sheets pipeline

Form submissions connect via Zapier capturing timestamp, email, prize won, and UTM source — enabling instant retargeting uploads and nurture triggers.

05
Nurture

Retargeting + email sequence

Leads flow into a 5-email nurture sequence and are added to Meta Custom Audiences for retargeting simultaneously.
